Issue 01

The Minds Behind
The Third Space

A Philippine Pop Culture project by seventeen students who believe cafes are worth writing about.

Editor-in-Chief

Lalaine Jannah dela Cruz

Editor-in-Chief · Web Developer · Photo Lead

College pushed me out of my comfort zone and into places I never thought to visit before. Cafes turned out to be some of the best ones. Every spot holds a memory, every favorite drink a little taste of a third home. Cafe hopping became a lifestyle, and what started as stepping out of comfort zones became something worth documenting and sharing. These spaces have a way of making you feel like there is always more to discover, always another place worth finding. That is what this project is really about. Welcome to the third place. There is a lot more out there, and we hope this gets you curious enough to go looking.
