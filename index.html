Editor's Note

A Note from the Editor

Every semester, we sit in the same classrooms, return to the same homes, and follow the same paths. But somewhere between the first sip of coffee and the hum of a playlist you didn't choose yourself, something shifts. The cafes of Lucena have become more than places to order drinks. They are where ideas are exchanged, where deadlines are survived, where friendships deepen over a shared table and the ambient warmth of something brewing.

This issue of The Third Space is our love letter to those rooms. To the third places that hold us when home feels too quiet and school feels too loud. We hope you find something of yourself in these pages — and maybe a new favorite corner of the city.

— Lalaine Jannah dela Cruz, Editor-in-Chief
